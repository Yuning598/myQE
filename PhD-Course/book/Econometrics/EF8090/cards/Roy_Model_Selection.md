# Roy Model Selection

Parent: [11_LATE_Roy_MTE](11_LATE_Roy_MTE)

:::{admonition} Definition (Roy selection model)
\[
Y=DY_1+(1-D)Y_0,
\qquad
D=1[U_D\le p(Z)].
\]

:::

Selection on gains means treatment choice is related to \(Y_1-Y_0\). The MTE framework summarizes how treatment effects vary across latent resistance \(U_D\)。
