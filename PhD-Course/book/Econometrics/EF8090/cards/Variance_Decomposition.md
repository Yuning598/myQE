# Variance Decomposition

Parent: [01_CEF_and_Linear_Projection](01_CEF_and_Linear_Projection)

:::{admonition} Lemma
Law of total variance
\[
\operatorname{Var}(Y)=E[\operatorname{Var}(Y\mid X)]+\operatorname{Var}(E[Y\mid X]).
\]

:::

The formula separates within-cell uncertainty from between-cell variation in conditional means. It is useful for understanding R-squared, treatment effect heterogeneity, and conditional variance models.
