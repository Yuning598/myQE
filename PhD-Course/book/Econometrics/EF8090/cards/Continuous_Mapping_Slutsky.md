# Continuous Mapping and Slutsky

Parent: [04_Asymptotic_Tools](04_Asymptotic_Tools)

:::{admonition} Lemma
CMT
If \(X_n\to_pX\) and \(g\) is continuous, then \(g(X_n)\to_pg(X)\)。

**Lemma:** Slutsky
If \(X_n\to_dX\) and \(Y_n\to_pc\), then \(X_nY_n\to_dXc\) and \(X_n+Y_n\to_dX+c\)。

:::

Use CMT for inverse matrices and Slutsky for replacing unknown variances by consistent estimates.
