## Chapter Structure

1. [Static Choice, Consumer Demand, and Integrability](01_Static_Choice_Consumer_Demand_and_Integrability.md): static choice, consumer demand, duality, integrability.
2. [Choice under Uncertainty](02_Choice_under_Uncertainty_and_State_Contingent_Equilibrium.md): VNM expected utility, risk aversion, Jensen.
3. [General Equilibrium and Welfare](03_General_Equilibrium_Production_and_Welfare.md): pure exchange, welfare theorems, Pareto efficiency.
4. [Theory of the Firm and Supply](04_Theory_of_the_Firm_and_Supply.md): production set, profit maximization, cost minimization.
5. [Production in Arrow-Debreu](05_Production_in_Arrow_Debreu.md): production economy, firms, household production.
6. [Equilibrium Under Uncertainty](06_Equilibrium_Under_Uncertainty.md): Arrow securities, state prices, complete markets, risk sharing.
7. [Principal-Agent Models, Moral Hazard, and Screening](07_Principal-Agent_Models_Moral_Hazard_and_Screening.md): incentive constraints, moral hazard, screening, single crossing.
