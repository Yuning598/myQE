# Envelope theorem in screening

导航：[Microeconomic index](../index.md)

Main note: [Microeconomic/07_Principal-Agent_Models_Moral_Hazard_and_Screening](../07_Principal-Agent_Models_Moral_Hazard_and_Screening.md)

For $U(\theta)=t(\theta)-\theta q(\theta)$, IC implies

$$
U'(\theta)=-q(\theta).
$$

Thus

$$
U(\theta)=U(1)+\int_\theta^1q(s)\,ds.
$$

With $IR$, $U(1)=0$. The rent term creates the virtual cost distortion

$$
\theta+\frac{F(\theta)}{f(\theta)}.
$$
