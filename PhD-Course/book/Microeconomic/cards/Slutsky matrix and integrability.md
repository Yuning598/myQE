# Slutsky matrix and integrability

导航：[Microeconomic index](../index.md)

Main note: [Microeconomic/01_Static_Choice_Consumer_Demand_and_Integrability](../01_Static_Choice_Consumer_Demand_and_Integrability.md)

Slutsky matrix:

$$
s_{ij} =
\frac{\partial x_i}{\partial p_j}
+
\frac{\partial x_i}{\partial w}x_j.
$$

Slutsky equation:

$$
s_{ij}=\frac{\partial h_i}{\partial p_j}.
$$

Integrability theorem: a demand function is generated by utility maximization iff it satisfies homogeneity of degree zero, Walras' law, and Slutsky symmetry plus negative semidefiniteness.
