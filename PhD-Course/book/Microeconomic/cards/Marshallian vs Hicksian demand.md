# Marshallian vs Hicksian demand

Main note: [Microeconomic/01_Static_Choice_Consumer_Demand_and_Integrability](../Microeconomic/01_Static_Choice_Consumer_Demand_and_Integrability)

Marshallian demand:

$$
x(p,w)\in\arg\max_x u(x)\quad\text{s.t. }p\cdot x\le w.
$$

Hicksian demand:

$$
h(p,\bar u)\in\arg\min_x p\cdot x\quad\text{s.t. }u(x)\ge \bar u.
$$

Key distinction:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Marshallian: }&\text{wealth fixed},\\
\text{Hicksian: }&\text{utility fixed}.
\end{aligned}
$$

Dual links:

$$
h(p,v(p,w))=x(p,w),\qquad x(p,e(p,\bar u))=h(p,\bar u).
$$
