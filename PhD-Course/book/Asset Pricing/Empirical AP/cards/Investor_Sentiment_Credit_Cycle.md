# Investor Sentiment and Credit Cycle

Source: EF8083 slides, pp. 500-520  
导航：[Asset Pricing index](../../index.md) · [Empirical AP MOC](../00-MOC_EF8083_Empirical_Asset_Pricing.md) · 来源：[09_Beliefs_Attention_Sentiment_Credit](../09_Beliefs_Attention_Sentiment_Credit)

Sentiment pricing:
$$
P_t=V_t+aS_t.
$$

If sentiment mean-reverts:
$$
E_t[S_{t+1}]=\phi S_t,\qquad 0<\phi<1,
$$
then:
$$
E_t[R_{t+1}]
\approx
\bar R-\frac{a(1-\phi)}{P_t}S_t.
$$

Credit spread decomposition:
$$
Spread_t=ExpectedLoss_t+RiskPremium_t+SentimentDiscount_t.
$$

High credit sentiment compresses spreads and can predict poor future credit returns.
