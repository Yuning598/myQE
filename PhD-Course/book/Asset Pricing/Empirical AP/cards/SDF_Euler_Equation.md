# SDF Euler Equation

Source: EF8083 slides, pp. 73-93  
导航：[Asset Pricing index](../../index.md) · [Empirical AP MOC](../00-MOC_EF8083_Empirical_Asset_Pricing.md) · 来源：[03_Consumption_Based_AP_Puzzles](../03_Consumption_Based_AP_Puzzles)

Representative investor:
$$
\max E_t\sum_{j=0}^{\infty}\beta^j u(C_{t+j}).
$$

SDF:
$$
M_{t+1}=\beta\frac{u'(C_{t+1})}{u'(C_t)}.
$$

Pricing equation:
$$
P_t=E_t[M_{t+1}X_{t+1}].
$$

Return form:
$$
E_t[M_{t+1}R_{t+1}]=1.
$$

Excess return form:
$$
E_t[M_{t+1}R_{t+1}^e]=0.
$$
