# SDF Euler Equation

Source: EF8083 slides, pp. 73-93  
Backlinks: [03_Consumption_Based_AP_Puzzles](../03_Consumption_Based_AP_Puzzles) | [05_Cross_Section_Factor_Models](../05_Cross_Section_Factor_Models)

Representative investor:
\[
\max E_t\sum_{j=0}^{\infty}\beta^j u(C_{t+j}).
\]

SDF:
\[
M_{t+1}=\beta\frac{u'(C_{t+1})}{u'(C_t)}.
\]

Pricing equation:
\[
P_t=E_t[M_{t+1}X_{t+1}].
\]

Return form:
\[
E_t[M_{t+1}R_{t+1}]=1.
\]

Excess return form:
\[
E_t[M_{t+1}R_{t+1}^e]=0.
\]
