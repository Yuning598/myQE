# Limited Attention and PEAD

Source: EF8083 slides, pp. 470-500  
导航：[Asset Pricing index](../../index.md) · [Empirical AP MOC](../00-MOC_EF8083_Empirical_Asset_Pricing.md) · 来源：[09_Beliefs_Attention_Sentiment_Credit](../09_Beliefs_Attention_Sentiment_Credit)

If only fraction $\lambda$ of information is incorporated each period:
$$
P_{t+j}-P_{t+j-1} =
\lambda(1-\lambda)^{j-1}s.
$$

Cumulative response:
$$
P_{t+k}-P_t =
s[1-(1-\lambda)^k].
$$

Unincorporated information:
$$
s(1-\lambda)^k.
$$

Implication: earnings news or other public signals can predict future returns when investors are inattentive.
