# Heterogeneous Beliefs and Short-Sale Constraints

导航：[Asset Pricing index](../../index.md) · 来源：[09_Beliefs_Attention_Sentiment_Credit](../09_Beliefs_Attention_Sentiment_Credit)

Two valuations:
$$
V^O>V^P.
$$

Without short-sale constraints, pessimists can short and price may reflect average beliefs. With constraints, pessimists' demand is bounded below:
$$
q^P\ge 0.
$$

Price can be set by optimists:
$$
P^{SSC}\approx V^O.
$$

Prediction: disagreement predicts low future returns especially when short-sale constraints are tight.
