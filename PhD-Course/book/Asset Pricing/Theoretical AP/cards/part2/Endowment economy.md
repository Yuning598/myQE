# Endowment economy

来源：[Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/02_Dynamic_Asset_Pricing#6.2 Lucas Tree Equilibrium](Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/02_Dynamic_Asset_Pricing#6.2 Lucas Tree Equilibrium)

返回：[Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/02_Dynamic_Asset_Pricing](Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/02_Dynamic_Asset_Pricing)

:::{admonition} Definition (Endowment Economy)
endowment economy 是一种 general equilibrium 框架：经济里给定一个外生的 aggregate endowment 过程，消费者的消费只能来自这个禀赋，资产价格由“如何在状态之间分配禀赋”决定。

在 Lucas tree model 里，股票正是这棵禀赋树的索取权；因此代表性消费者在均衡中消费整个 endowment，

$$
\begin{aligned}
c_t=\delta_t.
\end{aligned}
$$
:::

^def-endowment-economy

:::{admonition} Proposition
Lucas Tree Interpretation
若股票是对 aggregate endowment 的索取权，那么股票分红就是 endowment 本身，均衡定价就转化为对 endowment 过程的贴现定价。

:::
