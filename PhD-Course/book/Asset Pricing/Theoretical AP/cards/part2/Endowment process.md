# Endowment process

导航：[Asset Pricing index](../../../index.md) · 来源：[02_Dynamic_Asset_Pricing](../../02_Dynamic_Asset_Pricing.md)


:::{admonition} Definition (Endowment Process)
endowment process 描述 aggregate endowment 随时间如何演化。Lucas tree model 中常写为

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{d\delta_t}{\delta_t}=\mu_\delta\,dt+\sigma_\delta\,dZ_t.
\end{aligned}
$$
这里 $\delta_t$ 是总禀赋（也就是股票分红）的规模，$\mu_\delta$ 是平均增长率，$\sigma_\delta$ 是波动率。
:::

(def-endowment-process)=

:::{admonition} Proposition: Endowment Growth and Asset Prices
一旦 endowment process 给定，代表性消费者的消费、state-price density、risk-free rate 和股票价格都可以由它推出。

:::
