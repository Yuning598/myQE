# 组合选择问题 - 一阶条件

来源：[Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/01_Single_Period_Models#2.3 First-Order Condition (FOC)](Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/01_Single_Period_Models#2.3 First-Order Condition (FOC))

返回：[Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/01_Single_Period_Models](Asset Pricing/Theoretical AP/01_Single_Period_Models)

:::{admonition} Definition (Definition)
组合选择问题写作

$$
\max_{\{\theta_i\}} E[u(\tilde w)]
\quad \text{s.t.} \quad
\sum_{i=1}^n \theta_i p_i=w_0,
\qquad
\tilde w=\tilde y+\sum_{i=1}^n \theta_i \tilde x_i.
$$

**Proposition:** Proposition
一阶条件为

$$
E[u'(\tilde w)\tilde x_i]=\gamma p_i.
$$
等价地，

$$
E\!\left[\frac{u'(\tilde w)}{\gamma}\tilde x_i\right]=p_i.
$$

**Note:** Derivation
把预算约束代入，写成拉格朗日函数：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathcal{L}
&=E\!\left[u\!\left(\tilde y+\sum_{i=1}^n\theta_i\tilde x_i\right)\right]
-\gamma\left(\sum_{i=1}^n\theta_i p_i-w_0\right).
\end{aligned}
$$
对每个 $\theta_i$ 求偏导：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial \theta_i}
&=E\!\left[u'(\tilde w)\tilde x_i\right]-\gamma p_i=0.
\end{aligned}
$$
因而

$$
\begin{aligned}
E\!\left[\frac{u'(\tilde w)}{\gamma}\tilde x_i\right]=p_i.
\end{aligned}
$$
若改写成价格与 gross return：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\tilde R_i=\frac{\tilde x_i}{p_i}
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
E\!\left[\frac{u'(\tilde w)}{\gamma}\tilde R_i\right]=1.
\end{aligned}
$$
若存在无风险资产 $R_f$，则可以取差：

$$
\begin{aligned}
E\!\left[\frac{u'(\tilde w)}{\gamma}(\tilde R_i-R_f)\right]=0.
\end{aligned}
$$
这表示：边际效用加权后的超额收益在期望上为零。

:::

## 含义

边际效用加权后的未来支付，其现值等于价格。
