# 正态-正态更新 - 常数漂移学习

导航：[Asset Pricing index](../../index.md) · 来源：[04_Information_Beliefs_and_Learning](../04_Information_Beliefs_and_Learning.md)

## 核心推导

若

$$
dY_t=\tilde\mu\,dt+\sigma dB_t,
\qquad
\tilde\mu\sim N(\hat\mu_0^i,\sigma_i^2),
$$

则样本均值信号为

$$
\mu_t^Y:=\frac{Y_t-Y_0}{t}=\tilde\mu+\sigma\frac{B_t}{t},
$$

并满足

$$
\mu_t^Y\mid\tilde\mu\sim N\!\left(\tilde\mu,\frac{\sigma^2}{t}\right).
$$

正态-正态更新给出

$$
\tilde\mu\mid\mathcal F_t^Y\sim N(\hat\mu_t^i,\Sigma_t^i),
$$

其中

$$
\Sigma_t^i=\left(\frac{1}{\sigma_i^2}+\frac{t}{\sigma^2}\right)^{-1},
$$

$$
\hat\mu_t^i =
\frac{\frac{1}{\sigma_i^2}\hat\mu_0^i+\frac{t}{\sigma^2}\mu_t^Y}{\frac{1}{\sigma_i^2}+\frac{t}{\sigma^2}}.
$$

等价的 innovation 形式为

$$
d\hat B_t^i:=\frac{1}{\sigma}(dY_t-\hat\mu_t^i dt),
$$

从而

$$
\frac{d\Sigma_t^i}{dt}=-\frac{(\Sigma_t^i)^2}{\sigma^2},
\qquad
d\hat\mu_t^i=\frac{\Sigma_t^i}{\sigma}d\hat B_t^i.
$$
