# PhD Course Review Notes

This book organizes Finance PhD review notes across asset pricing, corporate finance, econometrics, microeconomics, and problem-set practice.

## Course Indexes

- [Asset Pricing](Asset%20Pricing/index.md): theoretical and empirical asset pricing, SDF, factor models, dynamics, and beliefs.
- [Corporate Finance](Corporate%20Finance/index.md): empirical corporate finance, financial policy theory, papers, and identification.
- [Econometrics](Econometrics/index.md): projection, OLS, asymptotics, IV, GMM, treatment effects, and panel data.
- [Microeconomics](Microeconomic/index.md): choice, risk, equilibrium, production, state prices, and information.
- [Problem Sets](ProblemSet/index.md): question-oriented review material.
- [Cross-Course Hub](Cross-Course/index.md): concept routes connecting the courses.
