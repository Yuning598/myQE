## 1. Core Question
How do heterogeneous tax clienteles and short-term arbitrage trading affect ex-dividend day pricing and trading volume?

## 2. Main Point
- Tax differences can create heterogeneous ex-dividend day preferences.
- Arbitrage trading and transaction costs shape both price drops and volume.
- The ex-date is therefore a joint test of clientele effects and trading frictions.
