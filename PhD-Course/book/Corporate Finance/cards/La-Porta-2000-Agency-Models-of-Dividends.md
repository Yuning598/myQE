# La Porta et al. (2000) - Agency Models of Dividends

:::{admonition} Note
**Question**

为什么公司要发 dividend？为什么不同国家的 dividend policy 差异很大？

**Note:** **Two agency models**

1. **Outcome model**
- minority shareholders 用法律/治理权利迫使 insiders 发 dividend
- 保护越强，越能“把钱拿出来”

2. **Substitute model**
- 在法律保护弱的国家，dividend 是一种 reputation mechanism
- 公司通过持续发 dividend 来建立“善待股东”的信誉

**Note:** **Predictions**

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Outcome model:} \quad
\text{shareholder protection} \uparrow
&\Longrightarrow \text{dividend payout} \uparrow \\
\text{and} \quad
\text{investment opportunities} \uparrow
&\Longrightarrow \text{dividend payout} \downarrow
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Substitute model:} \quad
\text{shareholder protection} \downarrow
&\Longrightarrow \text{dividend payout} \uparrow \\
\text{and} \quad
\text{investment opportunities} \uparrow
&\Longrightarrow \text{dividend payout} \uparrow \text{ (weak / ambiguous)}
\end{aligned}
$$

**Note:** **Main findings**

- better minority shareholder protection $\Longrightarrow$ higher dividend payout
- in better protection countries, fast growth firms pay lower dividends
- overall evidence supports the **outcome model** more than the **substitute model**
- no conclusive evidence on taxes
- signaling may matter on the margin, but is not the main cross-country force

**Note:** **Two ways the agency approach departs from MM**

1. dividend policy is **not independent** of investment policy
2. profits need not be allocated to shareholders **pro rata** because insiders and outsiders have conflict
