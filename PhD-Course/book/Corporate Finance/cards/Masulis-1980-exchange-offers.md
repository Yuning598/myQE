## Exchange offers

Masulis studies exchange offers as a capital structure change event.

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{exchange offers}
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
\text{debt for equity} \\
\text{debt for preferred} \\
\text{preferred for equity}
\end{aligned}
\right.
\end{aligned}
$$
