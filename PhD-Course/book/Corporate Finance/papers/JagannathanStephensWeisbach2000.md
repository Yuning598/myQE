## 1. Core Question
When do firms choose repurchases instead of dividends?

## 2. Main Point
- Repurchases are more consistent with temporary cash-flow increases.
- Dividends are more consistent with persistent earnings.
- Buybacks are therefore a more flexible payout choice than dividends.
