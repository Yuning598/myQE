# Reading Note: Allen, Bernardo and Welch (2000)

<a href="../01_Empirical_Corporate_Finance.html">Back to the main note</a>

## 1. Core Question
How do dividends interact with institutional investors, tax clienteles, and agency costs?

## 2. Main Point
- Firms can attract institutional investors by paying dividends.
- Institutional ownership can reduce agency costs through monitoring.
- The trade-off is that firms then have to smooth dividends to retain those investors.
