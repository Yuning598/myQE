## 1. Core Question
How far can ex-dividend day price drops deviate from the dividend amount once taxes and transaction costs are taken into account?

## 2. Main Point
- Ex-date price drops need not equal the cash dividend one-for-one.
- Trading costs and tax wedges create an arbitrage band.
