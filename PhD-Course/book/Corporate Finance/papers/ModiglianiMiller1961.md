# Reading Note: Modigliani and Miller (1961)

<a href="../01_Empirical_Corporate_Finance.html#sec-mm-dividend-irrelevance">Back to the main note</a>

## 1. Core Question
What is the value relevance of dividend policy and financing policy in a frictionless capital market?

## 2. Main Conclusion
- In perfect and efficient capital markets, firm value is pinned down by the firm’s free cash flow.
- Free cash flow is:
  - profits
  - plus depreciation
  - minus investment
- Therefore, dividend policy and financing policy do not affect firm value, as long as investment decisions and operating profits are unchanged.

## 3. Reading Frame
- This is the benchmark null for payout relevance.
- Any empirical dividend effect must come from frictions:
  - taxes
  - transaction costs
  - agency costs
  - information asymmetry
