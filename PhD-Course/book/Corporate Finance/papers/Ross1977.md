# Reading Note: Ross (1977)

## 1. Core Idea
Ross argues that financial structure can convey private information. If managers know firm quality and leverage is costly in distress, higher debt can signal higher quality because only good firms can credibly bear the expected bankruptcy cost.

## 2. Signaling Logic
- leverage is a **dissipative signal**: credibility comes from a real cost that bad firms cannot mimic cheaply
- low-quality firms avoid high debt because distress cost would be too large
- high-quality firms can choose higher leverage and separate themselves from lemons
