# Reading Note: Ma (2019)

## 1. Core Idea
Nonfinancial firms respond to relative valuation across debt and equity markets. When one side looks cheap or expensive, firms substitute between repurchases and issuance.

## 2. Course Use
- used in the main note as a market-timing / relative-misvaluation example
- complements the long-run timing literature on SEOs and repurchases
