# Reading Note: John and Knyazeva (2006)

<a href="../01_Empirical_Corporate_Finance.html">Back to the main note</a>

## 1. Core Question
How does governance quality affect dividend payout, and does better governance reduce the need for dividends as a substitute mechanism?

## 2. Main Point
- Better governance can reduce agency conflicts directly.
- When governance is strong, firms may rely less on dividends as a disciplinary device.
- This supports a substitution-style interpretation in which dividend demand falls as internal governance improves.
